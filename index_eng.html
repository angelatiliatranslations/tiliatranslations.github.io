Angela Brisotto | Linguist

Welcome to this space!

Since 2019, I have been guiding people who want to learn a new language with kindness and ingenuity, and I have been helping individuals and organisations convey their words and ideas to a public that speaks a different language.

Combining analysis, accuracy and wordsmithery, I:

  • offer Italian and English lessons;
  • translate texts from Polish and English into Italian;
  • revise translations and proofread texts written in Italian;
  • and transcribe audio and video materials into Italian.
"Languages. Let’s explore them together!"
